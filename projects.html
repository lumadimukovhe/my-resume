Financial Performance Reporting Project:

Led a critical project to report key financial performance indicators (KPIs) mandated by the National Treasury (Section 71) for evaluating all 257 South African Local municipalities. Gathered raw financial data from the Treasury website and applied data wrangling techniques using Python, transforming and preparing it for analysis, utilizing Azure cloud services for data storage, employing ETL processes to load and manage data efficiently. Conducted in-depth exploratory data analysis (EDA) on the data, identifying trends, outliers, and patterns to derive valuable insights.Designed and developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard integrating visualizations of KPIs, trend analysis, and performance comparisons. Collaborated closely with stakeholders to understand their requirements and refined the dashboard iteratively, ensuring its alignment with their needs.

Successfully presented the Power BI dashboard to key stakeholders, enabling informed decision-making and providing insights into the financial health of local municipalities.
